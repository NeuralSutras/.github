# NeuralSutras

**Practical AI for Everyone**

NeuralSutras is an AI education and media brand helping students, professionals, and business owners use AI to work smarter — without the hype.

## What we publish
- In-depth guides on using AI tools effectively
- Role-specific AI workflows (teachers, accountants, small business owners, and more)
- AI automation tutorials (n8n, agents, local AI)
- Honest, practical AI education — no clickbait
